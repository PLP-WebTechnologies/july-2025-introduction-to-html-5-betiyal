About Us 💡

ASKEMA TUBE is a dedicated online platform committed to sharing the rich spiritual heritage of the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church. We bring you authentic mezmur, kidassie, mahelet, and traditional instrumental worship such as masinko and begena. Our goal is to connect believers around the world through sacred music and teachings that uplift the soul and deepen faith.
